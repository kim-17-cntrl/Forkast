About Forkast

Forkast is made as a tool for UCSC students and community members yearning to eat sustainably and affordbly. We connect students to food resources, from pop-ups to farmers markets, and promote eco-conscious and nutricious food choices which offers an alternative to the neoliberal system of food shortages, and inflation.

Forkast is designed as a helpful tool for UCSC students who are eager to eat more sustainably and affordably. We aim to connect students with local food resources—ranging from campus pop-ups and community events to farmers markets—while promoting eco-conscious food habits that benefit both individuals and the environment.

Forkast also serves as a guide for students navigating local food systems, whether you're trying to budget for groceries, find healthier options, or discover new ways to eat with intention. From passionate food lovers to those just starting their sustainability journey, Forkast is here to support you in making smarter, greener, and more connected food choices.
