About Forkast

Forkast is made as a tool for UCSC students and community members yearning to eat sustainably and affordbly. We connect students to food resources, from pop-ups to farmers markets, and promote eco-conscious and nutricious food choices which offers an alternative to the neoliberal system of food shortages, and inflation.

Our mission is to empower our local community to engage with smaller food systems, eat for the enviornment, seasonally, and reduce waste. All the while, embracing the power of nourishing your body while helping the enviornment, as it should and can be.

From pasisonate concious eaters to those looking to support local food systems, Forkast is the tool to help unify these interests.
