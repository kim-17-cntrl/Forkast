About Forkast

Forkast is made as a tool for UCSC students yearning to eat sustainably and affordbly. We connect students to food resources, from pop-ups to farmers markets, and promote eco-conscious food choices.

Our mission is to empower communities to learn more about eating for the enviornment, eating seasonally, reduce waste, and embrace the power of nourishing your body while helping the enviornment.

From pasisonate eaters to people looking to support local food systems, Forkast is the tool to help.
